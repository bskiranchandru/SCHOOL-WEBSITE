About PES INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

-> Established in late 2005, our journey started in a small way and we got our first school recognized by Karnataka State Board in the year 2007 and today, under its aegis, we have three campuses and provides education from Daycare to University level to over 3000 students affiliated to State Board, ICSE We have sent out many batches of tenth State board and ICSE with cent percent result. We have been recognized as the best school in the taluk. In a matter of few years the academy has made its presence felt in the Sarjapura and beyond.

-> Our aim is to equip children in every area of their lives, whether it be academics, life skills or values. The Ultimate focus is on character building and overall development of a child to make them successful citizens.

-> We have rigorous academic programmes, complimented with an equally outstanding co-curricular and extra-curricular agenda for students which endow them with a much needed holistic education to give them a competitive edge over their counterparts.

-> We work closely with our parents to continually improve and excel in the performance of our duties.

-> The academy runs various exhibitions, talks, debates, excursions, educational events, shows and several sporting events both local and taluk level.

-> The academy has its own transport fleet with well trained Drivers and cleaners . All the buses are accompanied by attendants to take care of the kids

-> All the campuses are under CCTV surveillance, tight security and surrounded by security wall to ensure comfort, security and safety of students and make the parents feel safe.

-> Our Academy is Managed by Seema Educational Trust and Society. The Academy is turning out to be an ideal campus for quality education on modern lines.
